The technology

EMS technology devices use low-frequency electrical impulses to stimulate nerves. With the nerves are engaged, they send a message to the brain to engage surrounding muscle fibers, causing them to expand and contract. Just like a great workout, these contractions strengthen and build muscles to perform their best.
